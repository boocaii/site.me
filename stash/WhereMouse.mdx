[WhereMouse](https://apps.apple.com/app/wheremouse/id6529533408) is a native macOS app, which enables you press `Ctrl+Ctrl` to reveal your mouse's position.

![screenshot](/wheremouse-site-screenshot.png)

## Privacy
WhereMouse is an offline app, it does not use any network or access any other information.
